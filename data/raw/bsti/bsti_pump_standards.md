## Key Standards for Leo Pump Market Entry

### Mandatory BSTI Certification Required For:
1. **BDS 1082:1984** — Agricultural centrifugal pumps (primary Leo product line)
2. **Three-phase motor standard** — Motors paired with centrifugal pumps for agricultural use
3. **BDS 1145:1986** — If Leo offers vertical shaft pump variants

### Import Implications:
- All pump imports must comply with corresponding BDS standards
- BSTI certification mark required before sale
- Standards are available for purchase from BSTI (price groups A-K: ৳500-2,000)
- Adopted international standards (BDS ISO, BDS IEC) available at higher prices (৳1,500-3,000)
